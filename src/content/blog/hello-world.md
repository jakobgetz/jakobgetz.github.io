Welcome to my personal site! I am excited to share my thoughts here.

This website is designed with a strong separation of **design**, **functionality**, and **content**.
Here is how it is structured:

1. **Content**: All text and metadata live in markdown or JSON files inside `/src/content/`.
2. **Functionality**: Next.js App Router and TypeScript handles pages and routing.
3. **Design**: Native HSL-derived CSS custom variables govern layouts.

In the future, I plan to write about:
- Distributed consensus protocols.
- Next.js development tips.
- Compilers and WASM optimization techniques.

Stay tuned for more technical articles soon!
